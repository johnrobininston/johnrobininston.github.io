I am a 3rd year PhD student in the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at UC Santa Barbara.  My current research interests lie in the intersection of environmental science, financial mathematics and machine learning; specifically in California ground water management and trading systems.  

You can find summaries of my previous project work as well as course pages with materials for classes I have taught at UC Santa Barbara in the relevant tabs. 

I am also currently working on building and publishing a mathematics wiki using [obsidian publish](https://obsidian.md/publish) in collaboration with some of my PhD colleagues at UCSB, which I hope to release later this year.
